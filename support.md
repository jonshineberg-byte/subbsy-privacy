## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I add players to my team?**
Go to your team page and tap the + button to add players.

**Can I use Subbsy for both AFL and Soccer?**
Yes — select your sport when creating a team.

**My match data isn't saving. What should I do?**
Make sure you tap the save/done button after making changes. 
If the issue persists, try closing and reopening the app.

**How do I export a match summary?**
Open the match from Past Events, tap the share icon to 
export or share your match data.
