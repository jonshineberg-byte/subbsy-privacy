# Privacy Policy — Subbsy

**Last updated: April 2026**

Subbsy ("the app") is a match day management tool for sports coaches.

## Data Collection
Subbsy does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. 
All information entered into the app (teams, players, match data) 
is stored locally on your device only.

## Third-Party Services
Subbsy does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, 
or tracking services.

## Photos
If you choose to add a team photo, the app requests access to 
your photo library. Photos are stored locally on your device 
and are never uploaded or shared.
